DELIVERY

Where's my order?

1. Track your order

All you need to do is log in to 'My Account' and select the order you want to track.
Use the 'Track Parcel' button to track your package.

2. Check the estimated delivery date

You’ll find this date in your Order Confirmation email and by checking 'My Orders' in 'My Account'.

Please remember that this date is an estimate, so your order may arrive slightly before, on, or slightly after your estimated delivery date. To see the progress of your order, go to 'Track Order' in your account.

If the tracking doesn’t show any updates or is blank, please allow until your estimated delivery date for your order to be delivered. There may be a delay with your tracking being updated, but it should update soon.

If your items have been shipped in separate packages, you’ll receive separate shipping confirmation emails with different tracking numbers. This way you can track each parcel individually.

3. I'm still waiting for my order

Has your estimated delivery time passed? Please allow an extra few days for your order to be delivered (especially during busy times). Our carriers are always doing their best to get your order to you, so make sure you keep an eye on your tracking link for the latest updates.

If your order doesn’t arrive, please let us know within 30 days of your expected delivery date, and we’ll do our best to help you.

How does shipping with WEARO work in the US?

Shipping options

We use a large network of shipping partners and a range of options to make sure our customers are receiving their orders when they expect them!

Tracking my order

All packages sent to US are trackable. We’ll email you a link to your tracking information once your package has left the warehouse. You can also track your order by logging in to ‘'My Account'’ and viewing your most recent order.

If some of your items have been shipped in separate packages or from a different warehouse, you’ll receive separate dispatch emails with different tracking numbers. This way you can track each package individually.

If you ordered items that are shipped as kits, please allow until your estimated delivery date for your order to be delivered – the courier may sometimes be a delay with your tracking being updated.

FYI, if the delay is due to your order containing a ‘TS Ordered’, it means that we're getting your order together ready to be sent out. During busy times, this may mean your one order has longer than usual – don’t worry though, this shouldn't affect your estimated delivery date.

What happens on the day of delivery?

Please follow the tracking for your item to know exactly when to expect it. You’ll see live email updates with all tracking information once it's on the way.

You might need to sign for the package. If you're not in when your package arrives, it may be left in a safe location or with a neighbor, or it may be left with package info.

Ordered to a collection point? You’ll receive a message once your package is ready to be picked up.

Depending on where your package is coming from, delivery partners may offer slightly different delivery options. These might include changes to the recipient, delivery to a safe place, or collection from a specified location – so keep an eye on the updates and delivery contact for any info. You can also check your delivery email. You’ll know if your item is coming from a different delivery system when you see the link in the shipping update email, which leads to the tracking and shipping partner’s website or app.

Things to know

  • For security, we're unable to change the address your order is being sent to, so make sure you double-check the delivery address on your confirmation page before placing your order.
  • If you receive an email saying your package is on the way, it means your package has been processed for delivery.
  • We’re unable to reroute packages once in transit due to provided and checked progress, and you’ll lose the recovery. The delivery window we show at checkout is based on the estimated times from our carriers.
  • If your order contains items from both WEARO and Brand Partners, your order may arrive in more than one parcel. Items from WEARO and Partners may not be delivered separately.

How does delivery work with WEARO Brand Partners?

To provide our customers with a wider selection of products, we have partnered with some brands directly. On the product page, you'll see if an item is being sold by a WEARO Brand Partner in two ways:

  • Sold and Shipped by 'Brand Name' – means an item is being sold and shipped directly by a WEARO Brand Partner. Items shipped from a WEARO Brand Partner may arrive on a different day to the rest of your WEARO items – you’ll see the expected delivery date for each individual parcel at the checkout. For items sold and shipped by WEARO Brand partners, only Standard Delivery will be available at the checkout stage – Click & Collect and all other options are excluded. If you're subscribed to WEARO Premier, you won’t be able to make use of Premier Delivery, but you'll still get Premier benefits on parcels shipped from our WEARO warehouses. There are also some delivery restrictions for items sold and shipped by WEARO Brand Partners: if we can’t ship an item to your chosen delivery address, you’ll be advised at checkout.

  • Sold by 'Brand Name', Shipped by WEARO – means an item is being sold by a WEARO Brand Partner and shipped by WEARO. All delivery services we offer for all WEARO items will also be available for items sold by a WEARO Brand Partner and shipped by WEARO. You'll see the available shipping methods for your item(s) at the checkout stage. Find out more about our delivery options here. The WEARO Premier is available on items sold by a WEARO Brand Partner and shipped by WEARO.

You can only place an order for a WEARO Brand Partner product if you are a consumer. We are unable to sell WEARO Brand Partner products to businesses (and will therefore also be unable to supply VAT invoices in relation to any WEARO Brand Partner orders).

Item prices may differ as our Brand Partners set the price for the items they sell through the WEARO site. These prices may not align with the WEARO prices for the same item.
